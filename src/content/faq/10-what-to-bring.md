---
question: "What do I need to bring?"
category: "gear"
order: 10
---

Firearms for your division, all required ammunition (suggested maximum round count × 2), all lighting with fresh batteries and backups, eye protection, ear protection, footwear appropriate for uneven wooded terrain at night. Rifle sling, rigid pistol holster, water, food.

See the [First-Timer Guide](/first-timer) for a complete checklist.
