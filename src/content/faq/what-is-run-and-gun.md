---
question: "What is a run-and-gun biathlon?"
category: "general"
order: 1
---

A run-and-gun biathlon is a timed competition that combines distance running with live-fire shooting stages. At Twilight Biathlon, you run a 5K course through unlit Oklahoma terrain and engage four shooting stages along the route. Your final score is the combined total of your four stage times plus your course run time — lowest score wins.
