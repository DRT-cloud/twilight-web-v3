---
question: "What lighting do I need to bring?"
category: "gear"
order: 4
---

You are responsible for all illumination. The course is well-marked but completely dark. Bring a weapon-mounted light, a headlamp, and a backup flashlight at minimum. Fresh batteries and backups are essential — a light failure on course costs time.

NV competitors: white light is **not permitted** during Friday divisions.
