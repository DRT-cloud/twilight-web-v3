---
question: "How does payment work?"
category: "registration"
order: 10
---

Registration is a two-step process:

1. Register on PractiScore for the division(s) you want.
2. After PractiScore registration, you will receive an email with PayPal payment instructions.

**Your registration is not complete until PayPal payment is received.** Slots are not reserved by PractiScore signup alone.
