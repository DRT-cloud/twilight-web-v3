question: "What firearms are allowed?"
category: "gear"
order: 6
---

- **2Gun (Saturday):** center-fire rifle 5.45×39 or larger + center-fire pistol 9×19mm or larger.
- **PCC (Saturday):** pistol caliber carbine.
- **NV divisions:** follow the same firearm requirements as their Saturday counterparts.

All firearms semi-auto only. See the full [Rules page](/rules) for allowed calibers and prohibited ammunition.
