---
question: "What's the difference between NV and White Light divisions?"
category: "general"
order: 2
---

**NV divisions** run Friday night under night vision only. No white light is permitted anywhere on the course — not on weapons, not on helmets, not on phones. Friday is a true no-light environment.

**White Light divisions** run Saturday night. Competitors use handheld or weapon-mounted white lights to navigate and engage targets.

Each night has a **2Gun** division (rifle + pistol) and a **PCC** division (pistol-caliber carbine). A single competitor may enter a second division at the reduced $50 fee, max 2 divisions.
