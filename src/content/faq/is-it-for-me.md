---
question: "Is this event right for a first-timer?"
category: "general"
order: 3
---

If you are safe with firearms, comfortable running 3 miles on uneven terrain, and willing to do it at night, yes. The course is not technical trail running — it's Oklahoma fields, woods, and creek beds. Fitness matters, but course navigation and light discipline matter more.

First-timers regularly finish mid-pack. The staggered 5-minute release schedule means you are not racing other competitors in sight; you are racing the clock.
